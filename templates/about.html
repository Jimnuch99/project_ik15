What is this site about?

StudySpot is a interactive way of finding your favorite place to study. Just highlight your favorite Spot and let other students know that you've found the perfect study spot!

Site instructions:

Use the "Menu Sidebar" at the top left of your screen to navigate through the site.

  • 'Profile' will show your profile, posts, navigation to followers and following.
  • 'Upload' will let you upload your own StudySpot for other students to look at.
  • 'Settings' will let you change several profile or login texts.
